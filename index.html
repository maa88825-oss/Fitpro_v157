⚠️ Training Rules Enforced

✅ 80/20 Polarized: 80% easy volume, ≤20% hard
✅ ACWR ≤ 1.3: Acute load vs 4-week chronic
✅ +10% rule: Volume jump capped week-over-week
✅ 48-hour rule: Hard sessions never back-to-back
✅ Long run ≤ 30%: of weekly volume
✅ Deload every 4th week: Volume −40%
✅ Two-week taper: W11 −30%, W12 −50% + final test
✅ Mid-cycle test: 5K time trial at W6
